Entertainment

things to do

Exploration

The island offers numerous options for entertainment. Our most well-known? Beaches! We have miles of sandy beaches to enjoy the weather and participle in water activities! However, we have some rocky shores that are only accessible by boat for those desiring a more private area to vacation! We also have lush rainforests to hike and see a volcano! No matter which activity you choose, there is something for the entire family to enjoy!

Water Activities

The island provides several water activities that everyone will love! In addition, we offer chartered fishing tours to a few of the exotic fish the island reefs give life to, and even better: snorkeling!

Nightlife

When away from the beach and the water, the island offers a nightlife that will keep you busy! Wether it be dancing at the new dance club or drinking at one of the pubs, such as our microbrewery

Sightseeing

Spend your time in Taniti City with it's unique native architecture and nearly white sandy beaches that engulf Yellow Leaf Bay. The island also offers boat or bus tours of the island!

Other Activities

The island also offers other activities such as: movies, helicoptor rides, an arcade, art galleries, a museum, bowling and coming next year: golf!
